  • Your name and organization

We value discretion and confidentiality. Your inquiry will be handled with appropriate care.

Office Information

Cinder & Laurel Group operates as a private holdings company. We maintain a distributed team structure and do not offer walk-in services or public-facing office locations. All business is conducted by appointment and prior arrangement only.

Common Questions

Do you accept investment inquiries?

We occasionally consider opportunities that align with our investment criteria and operating principles. Please review our Portfolio page before reaching out.

Can I purchase products from your brands?

This site represents Cinder & Laurel Group as a holdings entity. For product inquiries, events, or consumer-facing services, please contact the specific operating brand directly.

Do you offer consulting or advisory services?

On a selective basis and within our areas of expertise. Inquiries regarding hospitality tobacco programs, regulatory compliance, or brand strategy may be directed to The Resident Tobacconist division.
