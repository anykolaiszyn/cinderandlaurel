The Resident Tobacconist

Craft Products

Cinder & Ash

An artisan pipe brand rooted in traditional craftsmanship and meticulous attention to materials. Each piece is designed and produced with an emphasis on durability, aesthetics, and the smoking experience. Our pipes honor centuries-old techniques while incorporating modern design sensibilities.

Cinder & Ash represents a commitment to the slow craft—small-batch production, hand-finishing, and a deep respect for the artisan tradition. We believe in objects that improve with age and use, becoming companions rather than commodities.

Investment Approach

What We Look For

While we do not actively seek external opportunities, ventures that align with our principles may occasionally be considered. We are drawn to businesses that demonstrate:

  • Sustainable competitive advantages rooted in craft, expertise, or unique market positioning
  • Clear regulatory compliance and ethical operating standards
  • Alignment with our focus on premium, artisanal, or specialty goods and services
  • Management teams committed to long-term value creation over quick exits
  • Business models that respect tradition while embracing thoughtful innovation
