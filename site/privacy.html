Our Commitment to Privacy

Cinder & Laurel Group ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you visit our website at cinderandlaurel.com (the "Site").

Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information

This Site is a simple static website hosted on GitHub Pages. We do not use analytics software, tracking cookies, or third-party advertising services. GitHub Pages may collect basic server logs (IP addresses, browser types, referring URLs) as part of standard hosting operations. Please refer to GitHub's Privacy Statement for details on their data practices.

Information You Provide

If you contact us via email at the address provided on our Contact page, we will receive and store the information you include in your message (name, email address, inquiry details). This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry and is not shared with third parties.

How We Use Information

Any information you voluntarily provide to us is used to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and communicate with you
  • Evaluate potential business relationships or partnership opportunities
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We do not engage in data mining, profiling, or behavioral advertising.

Cookies and Tracking

This Site does not use cookies or tracking technologies. We do not employ analytics platforms such as Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or similar services. Your browsing activity on this Site is not monitored or recorded by us.

Third-Party Links

Our Site may contain links to external websites or services (such as our operating brands' websites, if applicable). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect any information you provide. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Our Site is not directed to individuals under the age of 21. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately.
